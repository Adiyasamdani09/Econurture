About us

At Eco Nurture India Pvt Ltd quality is the topmost priority in the organization right from raw material, in-process disassemble, final inspection & also qualified vendors for supply of spares and consumables which support the final outcome of the product. Our aim is to consistently satisfy customers by disassembling and supplying high quality recycled part which can be re used as per customer requirements.

Eco Nurture India Pvt Ltd assurance to customers:

Disassemble from pretested devices

Assured chemical & mechanical properties as per customer requirement

Assured defect-free & internal soundness of product

accuracy

Part & tolerance in Parts as specified by the customer.

100% guarantee of high quality recycled parts

In order to ensure quality in all the stages from Disassemble, semi-finished condition & finished stage, we have the following testing equipment which is operated as per the quality documented procedure.

Testing Facility Available In Quality Dept

  • Optical emission spectrometer
  • Material identification through mobile spectrometer
  • 100% ultrasonic testing for internal defects
  • Radiation check meter
